<p style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #2c3e50;">As an educator, choosing the right tools and strategies to achieve your learning outcomes can be challenging, but doing so is essential to student success. This guide is designed to help you align educational technologies with specific learning types, supporting student engagement and deeper learning throughout your course.</p>

<div style="margin-top: 20px; font-size: 1.05em; color: #2c3e50;"><strong>As you explore this guide, consider the following questions:</strong>

    <ul style="margin-top: 10px; padding-left: 20px; list-style-type: disc;">

        <li>What types of learning and engagement do you want students to experience in order to achieve your course&rsquo;s intended outcomes?</li>

        <li>What challenges might prevent your students from achieving these outcomes?</li>

        <li>How do you want your students to engage with new concepts in your class?</li>

    </ul>

</div>

<p style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #2c3e50;">Now that you&rsquo;ve reflected on your learning goals and student needs, take some time to review the <span class="s1"><strong>Learning Types (</strong>Laurillard, 2012) </span><strong></strong>below. Each section is interactive. <strong>Click on a learning type</strong> to explore a curated list of recommended tools that support that specific kind of learning.</p>

<p style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #2c3e50;">Use this resource to experiment, reflect, and adapt your teaching strategies to create meaningful and impactful learning experiences.</p>
